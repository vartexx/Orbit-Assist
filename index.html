Chosen Vertical: Student Productivity

Orbit Assist

A smart, dynamic assistant for a final-year student or solo builder who is juggling classes, deadlines, meetings, and project work. Orbit Assist reads Google Calendar context, reasons over the day with Vertex AI, and turns intent into action with Google Workspace flows.

Google Services Calendar, Gmail, Tasks, Vertex AI, Maps, BigQuery
Primary persona Deadline-driven student builder
Core problem Too many commitments, not enough focused time

Challenge Alignment

This solution is intentionally designed around one clear persona and one clear workflow so the assistant behaves like a practical product, not a generic chatbot.

Persona

A final-year student, founder, or solo builder preparing for submissions, demos, interviews, and meetings in the same day.

Problem

Their day is fragmented by meetings and deadlines, so they need help deciding what to do next, when to focus, and how to follow up.

Google workflow

Google Calendar provides the live day context, Vertex AI generates the plan, Gmail drafts the follow-up, Google Tasks turns plans into trackable action items, BigQuery captures workflow analytics, and Google Maps links help with location-aware meetings.
